If the channels don't play on App Page, you can configure your browser to allow these specific streams to run:
1) Open your App Page website link in Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge.
2) Click the icon to the left of the website address (it looks like a padlock, settings sliders, or an info circle next to the URL bar).
3) Click on Site settings.
4) Scroll down the list of permissions until you find Insecure content.
5) Change the dropdown menu next to it from Block (default) to Allow.
6) Go back to your website and reload/refresh the page.
